Incomplete Enquiries

Enquiries that have been created but not fully completed for one reason or another are saved but with a status of ‘incomplete’. This could be for a number of reasons, maybe the customer gave up half way through completing an online form, or the online form encountered an error and the customer couldn’t continue.

Incomplete enquiries may have some information missing; therefore until an enquiry has been edited and ensure that all information needed has been gathered then you will not be able to take any actions on that enquiry.

‘Incomplete’ enquiries are only kept on the system for 24 hours after the time they were created before they are automatically archived. To prevent this please follow the steps below to complete the enquiry.

Completing an enquiry

 

1.       Locate the enquiry in Clic and open the enquiry details screen (below).

2.       A message will be displayed at the top of the screen to warn that the enquiry is not complete

3.       Click the ‘Complete’ button



4.        This will open the edit screen where you can add all the missing information

5.       Once you have checked all the information is complete, save the enquiry. This will set the enquiry as ‘Complete’ and it will not be archived.
